# Cincinnati Reds - Great American Ballpark
Great American Ballpark replaced Riverfront Stadium in Cincinnati.  It is located on the Ohio River in Downtown Cincinnati. The stadium pays tribute to the Red's history with statutes depicting many of the great players in team history throughout the stadium.  This is another stadium that I have yet to go to despite driving through the city on a couple occasions.

> "Rounding third and heading for home." - Joe Nuxhall

This is a famous quote from legendary player and announcer, these words are etched in giant letters into the brick office building overlooking the park.

![[great-american-ballpark.jpeg]]

## Design and Features
* **Power stacks, steamboat smokestacks shoot fireworks, mist, and lightup when the Reds win or hit a homerun**
* **Hitter friendly dimensions, only 328 to left field wall and 325 to right field**
* **Capacity 43,500 fans**

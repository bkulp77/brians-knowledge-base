# New York Mets - Citi Field
Citi Field opened in 2009 and has a modern but retro design to emulate Ebbets Field.  It has a red brick, limestone and granite facade with arched entry ways. It is located in Queens NY near the site of the old Shea Stadium and the site of the US Tennis Open site.

> "A life is not important except in the impact it has on other lives" - Jackie Robinson

This quote is engraved in the upper ring of Jackie Robinson Rotunda which serves as the main entrance to the ballpark.

![[citi-field.jpeg]]

## Design and Features
* **Green architecture: facility built with eco-friendly elements, permeable pavers, high mast lighting, and dark sky compliant**
* **Open concourses: designed as a 360 degree open air concourse to allow continuous views while walking the stadium**
* **Vertically stacked seating: this creates a steep seating bowl but reduces obstructed views**
* **Capacity 41,922 fans**

### NL East Ball Park Capacity Leaders

# Assignment 02: Analyzing an Organizing System
## What are the primary resources being organized?

I went to the NCIS TV show wikipedia page https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/NCIS_(TV_series), which contains physical, digital and abstract resourses. The main resourses are digital assets that contains a list of all NCIS cast members and who they play which link to wikipedia pages about both the actor and the character. There is also a listing of all episodes and seasons with the dates that they aired, however there is no recap of those episodes.

## What are the primary interactions the system supports?

Browsing and clicking on links within the page are the primary interaction.  You can see the list of actors and episodes from the main page and then click on those to get more information on what you are looking for.  You can also use the search function but it may take you away from the main NCIS page such as if I wanted more information on Season 2 i could search NCIS Season 2 and get a synopsis of the season.

## What classification system does it use?

The classificaiton system is a hybrid but the main page is hierarchical.  To get to NCIS Season 2 episode 1 you would have to go to the main NCIS page and then select the original series as there are many spinoffs listed on the page and then drill down into the seasons and then episodes.  It also uses a faceted system at the bottom of the page allowing you to search by director, actor, year and other tagging information that you can filter.

## How are the relationships between resources represented?

The relationships on the page are both structural and semantic.  The infoboxes on the actor profile pages show when the character premiered, last appearance, who portrayed them, family tree, and other background information on the character.

The structural relationships are represented by hyperlinks which have bolded text and you can click on those links to go to more information and additional pages.  One example is that the text mentions that NCIS is based out of the Washington Navy Yard which is bolded and you can click on that to got the Wikipage about the Navy Yard.
